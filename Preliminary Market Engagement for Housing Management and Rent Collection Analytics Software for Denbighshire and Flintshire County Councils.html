Project Requirements Overview

The councils are seeking a ready-made Housing Management Analytics Software to support:

  • Rent Collection: Predict arrears, manage cases, automated messaging, reporting.
  • Housing Management: Monitor tenancy engagement, track complaints, flag unacted issues.
  • Repairs Monitoring: Track repairs, identify repeat issues, predict future repairs, monitor damp & mould.
  • Messaging: Text, multimedia messaging, automated campaigns, integrated with Capita Open Housing.
  • Key Requirement: Off-the-shelf software, quick implementation, full integration with Capita Open Housing.
