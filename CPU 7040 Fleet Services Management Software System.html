Fleet Management System Proposal

Introduction

Our company is pleased to present a solution for the Council's Fleet Management needs. We understand the Council requires a secure, cloud-hosted, web-based platform that can manage all fleet assets, workshops, maintenance schedules, and provide a full audit trail.

Challenges We Address

  • Efficient management of all fleet vehicles and assets.
  • Planning and scheduling of periodic maintenance and inspections.
  • Tracking unplanned repairs and workshop jobs.
  • Monitoring technician hours and parts usage.
  • Maintaining a full audit trail for compliance and accountability.

Proposed Solution

We propose a modern Fleet Management System that is workshop-centric and designed to streamline operations across all Council workshops. The key features include:

  • Cloud-based and web-accessible from any device.
  • Full lifecycle management for all fleet vehicles and external assets.
  • Comprehensive workshop management: job cards, technician hours, and parts usage tracking.
  • Planned and unplanned maintenance scheduling.
  • Secure audit trails to ensure compliance and transparency.
  • Scalable architecture to support future expansion and integration.

Implementation Approach

  1. Discovery and requirement confirmation with Council staff.
  2. System configuration and setup based on current workflows.
  3. Data migration from existing systems.
  4. Staff training for all workshops and fleet managers.
  5. Go-live support and ongoing maintenance.

Why Choose Us

  • Proven expertise in delivering cloud-based fleet management solutions.
  • Experienced in integrating workshop operations into fleet software.
  • Reliable support and ongoing maintenance for long-term success.
  • Commitment to delivering a secure, user-friendly, and efficient system.
